<p><p>The wheelchair anti-tippers market comprises two main types: forward anti-tippers and rear anti-tippers. Forward anti-tippers are designed to prevent the wheelchair from tipping over when moving downhill or over obstacles, enhancing user safety during forward motion. Rear anti-tippers, on the other hand, help stabilize the wheelchair from falling backward, especially when navigating inclines or uneven surfaces. Both types are essential for promoting the safety and stability of wheelchair users, allowing for more confident mobility in various environments.</p></p>
