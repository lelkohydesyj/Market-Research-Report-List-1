<p><ul><li>Mechanically Held</li><li>Electrically Held</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Multi-Pole Lighting Contactors market is categorized into two main types: mechanically held and electrically held contactors. Mechanically held contactors utilize physical mechanisms, like latching and levers, to maintain their position without continuous power, enhancing energy efficiency and reducing heating. In contrast, electrically held contactors require constant electrical input to remain closed, providing quick response times and ease of automation. Both types serve to control lighting systems, contributing to energy management and operational flexibility in commercial and industrial settings.</p></p>
