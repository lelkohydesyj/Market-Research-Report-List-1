<p><p>Bone growth stimulation devices are categorized into three main types: Inductive Coupling Therapy, which uses electromagnetic fields to promote bone healing; Capacitive Coupling Therapy, which employs electrical stimulation to enhance cellular activity and repair; and Combined Therapy, which integrates both inductive and capacitive methods for a more comprehensive approach. These devices are designed to support the healing of fractures and bone modifications, particularly in patients with delayed or non-unions, enhancing overall recovery and bone health.</p></p>
