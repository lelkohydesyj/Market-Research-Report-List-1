<p><p>The cardiovascular disease monitoring and diagnostic devices market includes several key types that enhance cardiac health assessment. ECG systems capture heart activity through electrodes, providing real-time data. Event monitors are portable devices that record heart rhythms during specific symptoms. Holter monitors are wearable ECG devices for continuous heart monitoring over extended periods. Implantable loop recorders are small devices implanted under the skin to monitor heart rhythms over months. ECG management systems streamline data storage and analysis for efficient cardiac care.</p></p>
