<p><p>The rubber additives masterbatch market comprises various types, primarily classified as EPDM-based, NBR-based, and others. EPDM-based masterbatches utilize ethylene propylene diene monomer rubber, offering superior weather resistance and elasticity, making them ideal for automotive and industrial applications. NBR-based masterbatches, derived from nitrile butadiene rubber, excel in oil resistance and are commonly used in seals and gaskets. The "Others" category includes various specialty masterbatches tailored for specific properties, such as improved durability, color retention, or processing efficiency, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
