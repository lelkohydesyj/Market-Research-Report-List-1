<p><p>The semiconductor diffusion equipment market comprises several key types, including oxidation furnaces, annealing furnaces, and rapid thermal processing (RTP) furnaces. Oxidation furnaces are used to grow oxide layers on silicon wafers, essential for dielectric applications. Annealing furnaces facilitate the relaxation of stress and activation of dopants after implantation. RTP furnaces enable precise temperature control for rapid thermal processing, ensuring quick and uniform diffusion. These equipment types are critical for various semiconductor fabrication processes, enhancing performance and reliability.</p></p>
