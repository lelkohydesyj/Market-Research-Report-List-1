<p><p>The heat shield market is primarily divided into two types: rigid heat shields and flexible heat shields. Rigid heat shields are constructed from hard materials that provide structural support and are often used in high-temperature applications like aerospace and automotive industries. In contrast, flexible heat shields are made from adaptable materials that can conform to various shapes and surfaces, offering insulation and protection while remaining lightweight. Both types serve critical roles in thermal management and protection against extreme temperatures.</p></p>
