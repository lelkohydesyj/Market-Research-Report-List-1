<p><p>The heavy mining dump truck market is primarily categorized into articulated and rigid trucks. Articulated trucks feature a hinge between the cab and the dump box, allowing for better maneuverability on rough terrain, making them ideal for challenging mining environments. Rigid trucks, on the other hand, have a fixed structure that offers higher load capacities and greater stability on flat surfaces, suited for transporting heavy materials. Both types are critical for efficient material handling in mining operations, each serving unique operational needs.</p></p>
