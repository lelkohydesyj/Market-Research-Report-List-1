<p><p>The flexible circuit board market includes various types tailored for different applications. Sculptured boards have a unique 3D shape for specific designs. Multi-layer boards consist of several layers for complex circuitry, improving performance in compact spaces. Rigid-flex boards combine flexible and rigid sections, offering versatility and durability for intricate designs, particularly in consumer electronics and automotive industries.</p></p>
