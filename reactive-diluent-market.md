<p><p>The reactive diluent market consists of three main types: aliphatic, aromatic, and cycloaliphatic. Aliphatic reactive diluents are characterized by their straight or branched chain structures, offering low viscosity and good adhesion properties. Aromatic diluents, containing ring structures, provide enhanced performance in terms of chemical resistance and curing speed. Cycloaliphatic diluents, with their cyclic structures, combine attributes of both aliphatic and aromatic types, typically delivering superior clarity and UV stability, making them suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
