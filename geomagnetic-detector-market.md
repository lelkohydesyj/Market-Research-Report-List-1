<p><p>The geomagnetic detector market can be categorized into three main types: on-surface, buried, and semi-buried detectors. On-surface detectors are used for applications requiring direct ground analysis, providing easy accessibility and quick deployment. Buried detectors operate below the surface, ideal for detecting subterranean anomalies without disturbing the ground above. Semi-buried detectors are positioned partially underground, offering a balance between depth sensitivity and surface accessibility, making them suitable for varied geological and environmental conditions. Each type has unique applications based on detection needs.</p></p>
