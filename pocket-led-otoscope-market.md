<p><p>The Pocket LED Otoscope market includes two primary types: Halogen Lamp and LED Lamp otoscopes. Halogen Lamp otoscopes utilize traditional halogen bulbs, offering bright illumination and accurate color representation, but can generate more heat and have shorter lifespans. In contrast, LED Lamp otoscopes employ energy-efficient LEDs, providing longer-lasting light with lower heat emission. The shift towards LED technology is driven by advancements in portability, battery life, and overall brightness, enhancing diagnostic capabilities in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
