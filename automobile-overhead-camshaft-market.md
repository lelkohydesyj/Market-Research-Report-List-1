<p><ul><li>SOHC</li><li>DOHC</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile overhead camshaft market is primarily categorized into three types: Single Overhead Camshaft (SOHC), Dual Overhead Camshaft (DOHC), and others. SOHC features one camshaft per cylinder head, typically offering simplicity and lower manufacturing costs, suitable for smaller engines. DOHC comprises two camshafts per head, allowing for enhanced airflow and performance, making it popular in high-performance and modern vehicles. The "Other" category includes various specialized configurations and advanced systems, catering to niche markets and specific engine designs.</p></p>
