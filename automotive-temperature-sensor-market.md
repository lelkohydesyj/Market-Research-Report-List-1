<p><strong>The Automotive Temperature Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermistor Temperature Sensor</li><li>Thermocouple Temperature Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive temperature sensor market comprises various types, primarily thermistor and thermocouple sensors. Thermistor temperature sensors use ceramic materials to change resistance with temperature variations, offering high sensitivity and precision, making them suitable for engine and cabin temperature monitoring. In contrast, thermocouple temperature sensors consist of two dissimilar metals generating a voltage proportional to temperature differences, suitable for high-temperature applications. Both types are essential for vehicle performance, safety, and efficiency, catering to the industry's growing demand for reliable temperature monitoring systems.</p></p>
