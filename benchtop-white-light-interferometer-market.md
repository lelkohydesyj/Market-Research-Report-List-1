<p><p>The benchtop white light interferometer market comprises two main types: single-path interferometers and multipath interferometers. Single-path interferometers use a single optical path to measure surface topography or thickness, ensuring high resolution with minimal complexity. In contrast, multipath interferometers employ multiple optical paths, enhancing measurement capabilities, including measuring larger areas and improving sensitivity. Both types cater to specific applications in industries such as manufacturing, optics, and materials science, making them essential tools for precision measurement and quality control.</p></p>
