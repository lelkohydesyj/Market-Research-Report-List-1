<p><p>The EV charging meter market is categorized into three main types: single phase energy meters, three phase energy meters, and DC energy meters. Single phase meters are typically used for residential charging, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Three phase meters support higher power demands, making them ideal for commercial and public charging stations. DC energy meters are designed for fast charging applications, providing high efficiency and rapid energy transfer for electric vehicles. Each type serves distinct needs in the growing EV infrastructure.</p></p>
