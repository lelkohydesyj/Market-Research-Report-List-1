<p><p>The plastic pipe jointing and welding market encompasses various techniques for connecting pipes, each suited to specific applications. Mechanical fastening involves using clamps or connectors to securely join pipes without altering their structure. Adhesive bonding uses specialized adhesives to create a strong, chemical bond between pipe surfaces, ideal for permanent connections. Welding, particularly fusion welding, entails melting the pipe edges to form a seamless joint, offering high strength and leak resistance. These methods cater to diverse industrial and infrastructural needs.</p></p>
