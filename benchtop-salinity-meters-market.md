<p><p>Benchtop salinity meters are categorized into single channel, dual channel, and multi-channel types based on their measurement capacity. Single-channel meters focus on measuring salinity from one sample at a time, ideal for simple applications. Dual-channel meters allow the simultaneous measurement of two samples, enhancing efficiency. Multi-channel meters, designed for advanced applications, enable the measurement of multiple samples simultaneously, catering to high-throughput environments. These variations cater to diverse user needs in laboratories and industrial settings, balancing precision with workflow efficiency.</p></p>
