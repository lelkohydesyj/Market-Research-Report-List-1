<p><p>Variable Valve Timing (VVT) is an automotive technology that optimizes engine performance by adjusting the timing of the valve opening and closing. There are two main market types: Discrete (Stepped) Adjustment and Continuous (Infinite) Adjustment. Discrete systems offer fixed timing adjustments at specific intervals, enhancing efficiency and power but with limited flexibility. In contrast, continuous systems provide real-time, smooth adjustments across a range of operating conditions, resulting in improved fuel efficiency, reduced emissions, and better overall engine performance.</p></p>
