<p><strong>The Antacid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Proton Pump Inhibitor</li><li>H2 Antagonist</li><li>Acid Neutralizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antacid market consists of three main types: Proton Pump Inhibitors (PPIs), H2 Antagonists, and Acid Neutralizers. PPIs reduce stomach acid production by inhibiting the proton pumps in stomach lining, offering long-term relief for conditions like GERD. H2 Antagonists block histamine receptors to decrease acid secretion, providing moderate relief. Acid Neutralizers, commonly made of compounds like magnesium or calcium, directly neutralize stomach acid for immediate relief. Together, these products cater to various patient needs and preferences in managing acid-related disorders.</p></p>
