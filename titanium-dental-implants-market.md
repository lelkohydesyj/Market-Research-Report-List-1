<p><strong>The Titanium Dental Implants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Endosteal Implants</li><li>Subperiosteal Implants</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The titanium dental implants market is primarily categorized into three types: endosteal implants, subperiosteal implants, and others. Endosteal implants are placed directly into the jawbone, providing a strong foundation for artificial teeth. Subperiosteal implants are positioned under the gum but above the jawbone, suitable for patients with insufficient bone height. "Others" encompasses alternative designs and materials not fitting neatly into these categories, including mini implants and zygomatic implants, catering to diverse patient needs and anatomical conditions.</p></p>
