<p><strong>The Multi-layer Parquet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glued</li><li>Floating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multi-layer parquet market is typically categorized into two types: glued and floating. The glued market involves parquet flooring where individual pieces are adhered to the subfloor using strong adhesives, providing stability and durability. This type is ideal for areas with high foot traffic. Conversely, the floating market allows planks to interlock without being attached to the subfloor, facilitating easier installation and removal. This type offers flexibility and is suited for DIY projects and temporary settings.</p></p>
