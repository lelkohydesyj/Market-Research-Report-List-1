<p><p>Grapeseed carrier oil is available in two primary types: solvent extracted and cold pressed. Solvent extracted grapeseed oil involves using chemical solvents to separate the oil from the seeds, resulting in a higher yield but potentially leaving residue. Cold pressed grapeseed oil is derived by mechanically pressing the seeds without heat, preserving its natural properties and nutrients. Each type caters to different market demands, with cold pressed often favored for cosmetic and culinary applications due to its purity and quality.</p></p>
