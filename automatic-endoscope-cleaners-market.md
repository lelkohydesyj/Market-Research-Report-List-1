<p><ul><li>Single Chamber Cleaners</li><li>Multi Chamber Cleaners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic endoscope cleaners market features two primary types: single chamber cleaners and multi chamber cleaners. Single chamber cleaners utilize a single compartment to perform cleaning, disinfection, and rinsing of endoscopes, offering a compact and efficient solution for facilities with limited space. In contrast, multi chamber cleaners incorporate multiple compartments for sequential cleaning processes, allowing for thorough disinfection and improved workflow efficiency in larger medical settings. Both types enhance infection control and ensure the safe reuse of endoscopic instruments.</p></p>
