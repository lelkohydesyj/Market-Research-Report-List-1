<p><ul><li>Solid Particles</li><li>Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The specialty zeolites market is divided into two primary types: solid particles and powder. Solid particles typically feature larger granules that offer enhanced stability and durability, making them ideal for applications like catalysis and adsorption in various industrial processes. On the other hand, powder zeolites have a finer particle size, which increases their surface area and reactivity, making them suitable for applications in detergents, agriculture, and pharmaceuticals. Both forms cater to distinct industry needs, enhancing efficiency and performance in their respective functions.</p></p>
