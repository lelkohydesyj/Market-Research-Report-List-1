<p><p>The alarm detector market can be categorized into active and passive detectors. Active detectors emit signals, such as sound or light, and require a response to confirm or trigger an alarm, often used in systems where immediate attention is necessary. Passive detectors, on the other hand, monitor changes in their environment, such as heat or motion, and alert users when a certain threshold is breached. Both types serve crucial roles in enhancing safety and security across various applications.</p></p>
