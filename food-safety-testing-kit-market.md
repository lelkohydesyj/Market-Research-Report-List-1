<p><p>The food safety testing kit market encompasses various types, including PCR-based, immunoassay-based, and others. PCR-based kits utilize polymerase chain reaction technology to detect and quantify specific pathogens or contamination in food products. Immunoassay-based kits rely on antigen-antibody reactions to identify contaminants, such as allergens or toxins, offering rapid results. Other types may include colorimetric tests, biosensors, or culture methods. Each type addresses specific food safety needs, ensuring compliance with regulations and protecting consumer health.</p></p>
