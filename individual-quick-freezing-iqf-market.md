<p><p>Individual Quick Freezing (IQF) is a method used to freeze food rapidly, preserving texture, flavor, and nutritional value. The market is primarily divided into two types: Mechanical IQF and Cryogenic IQF. Mechanical IQF uses cold air circulation to freeze items, making it suitable for a variety of products like fruits and vegetables. In contrast, Cryogenic IQF employs liquid nitrogen or carbon dioxide for ultra-fast freezing, ideal for delicate items such as seafood and prepared meals, ensuring minimal cellular damage and superior quality.</p></p>
