<p><p>Ship trim optimization systems enhance vessel performance by adjusting their buoyancy and weight distribution. Modular optimization focuses on specific aspects of a ship, like cargo load and ballast adjustments, allowing for targeted improvements without overhauling the entire system. In contrast, holistic optimization evaluates the ship's entire operational profile, integrating various parameters such as speed, sea conditions, and fuel efficiency, ensuring a comprehensive approach to performance enhancement and maximizing overall efficiency. Both methods aim to reduce fuel consumption and operational costs.</p></p>
