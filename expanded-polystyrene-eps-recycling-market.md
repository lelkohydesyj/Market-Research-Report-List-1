<p><p>The Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) recycling market encompasses three main types: material recycling, chemical recycling, and thermal recycling. Material recycling involves physically processing EPS waste into usable products, like insulation or packaging materials. Chemical recycling breaks down EPS into its original monomers, allowing for the creation of new polystyrene materials. Thermal recycling, meanwhile, involves incinerating EPS to convert it into energy while managing emissions. Each method aims to reduce waste and promote sustainability in EPS usage.</p></p>
