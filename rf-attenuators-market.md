<p><p>The RF attenuators market consists of fixed and variable attenuators, each serving distinct applications in signal management. In contrast, variable attenuators enable adjustable attenuation levels, offering greater flexibility for dynamic environments where signal power needs to be tailored. Both types play crucial roles in telecommunications, broadcasting, and electronic testing, ensuring optimal signal integrity and performance.</p></p>
