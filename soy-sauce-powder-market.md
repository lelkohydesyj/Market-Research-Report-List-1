<p><p>The soy sauce powder market is primarily divided into two types: fermented soy sauce powder and roasted soy sauce powder. Fermented soy sauce powder is produced through the traditional fermentation process, resulting in a rich umami flavor suitable for various cuisines. In contrast, roasted soy sauce powder is created by roasting soybeans before processing, which imparts a unique, nutty taste. Both types are used as seasoning agents in cooking, snacks, and sauces, catering to diverse culinary preferences.</p></p>
