It includes three primary modes: Airways, where refrigerated aircraft swiftly transport products over long distances; Roadways, which utilize temperature-controlled trucks for local and regional distribution; and Seaways, which employ refrigerated ships for bulk transportation across oceans. Each mode ensures the integrity and safety of food items throughout the supply chain, catering to different distances and scales of delivery.</p></p>
