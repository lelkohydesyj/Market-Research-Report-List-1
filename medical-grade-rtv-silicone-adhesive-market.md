<p><p>The medical-grade RTV silicone adhesive market is categorized into single-component and double-component adhesives. Single-component silicone adhesives cure upon exposure to moisture, simplifying application and storage. They are ideal for various medical devices due to their ease of use. In contrast, double-component silicone adhesives require a specific ratio of components to initiate curing, offering enhanced durability and performance. These adhesives are often used in applications demanding stronger bonding and resistance to extreme conditions, making them suitable for various medical applications.</p></p>
