<p><strong>The Complementary Metal Oxide Semiconductor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PMOS</li><li>NMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Complementary Metal Oxide Semiconductor (CMOS) market primarily consists of two types: PMOS (P-channel MOSFET) and NMOS (N-channel MOSFET) transistors. PMOS transistors use positively-doped semiconductors and are ideal for low-power applications, offering high noise margins. NMOS transistors, on the other hand, are faster and more efficient, utilizing negatively-doped materials, making them suitable for high-speed circuits. Together, these technologies enable the design of power-efficient and high-performance devices, fueling advancements in the electronics industry.</p></p>
