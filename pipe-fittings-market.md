<p><ul><li>Metal Pipe Fittings</li><li>Plastic Pipe Fittings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pipe fittings market is categorized primarily into metal and plastic fittings. Metal pipe fittings, made from materials like stainless steel, copper, and aluminum, are known for their durability and high resistance to temperature and pressure, making them ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, plastic pipe fittings, typically made from PVC, CPVC, or PEX, are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and cost-effective, suitable for residential plumbing and irrigation systems. Each type serves distinct needs based on application, environment, and budget considerations.</p></p>
