<p><ul><li>Uni-polar TVS</li><li>Bi-polar TVS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TVS diode market is categorized into two main types: uni-polar and bi-polar TVS diodes. Uni-polar TVS diodes are designed to protect against voltage transients in one direction, making them ideal for DC applications. Bi-polar TVS diodes can handle transients in both directions, providing enhanced protection for AC applications and circuits sensitive to both positive and negative voltage spikes. Each type serves specific applications, contributing to their distinct market segments driven by varying electronic protection needs.</p></p>
