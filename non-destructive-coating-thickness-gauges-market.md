<p><p>Non-destructive coating thickness gauges utilize various technologies to measure the thickness of coatings without damaging the substrate. Magnetic and eddy current techniques are common for measuring non-conductive coatings on ferrous and non-ferrous metals respectively. Ultrasonic technology is ideal for thicker coatings and can measure materials like plastics and ceramics. X-ray technology provides precise thickness measurements in complex environments but requires more stringent safety measures. Other methods encompass a range of specialized techniques tailored to specific applications and materials.</p></p>
