<p><p>In the automotive market, blockchain can be categorized into three main types. Private blockchain, in contrast, restricts access to certain parties, such as suppliers and manufacturers, enhancing confidentiality and control over data. Each type serves distinct needs within the automotive ecosystem.</p></p>
