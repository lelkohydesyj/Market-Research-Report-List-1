<p><ul><li>Batch Feed Type</li><li>Continuous Feed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The in-sink disposal market is primarily divided into two types: batch feed and continuous feed models. Batch feed disposals require users to load food waste into the unit and activate it, ensuring safety as it operates only when the lid is closed. In contrast, continuous feed disposals allow users to add waste continuously while running, providing convenience during meal prep. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with batch feed favoring safety and continuous feed focusing on efficiency.</p></p>
