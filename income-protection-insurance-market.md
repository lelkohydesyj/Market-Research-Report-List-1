<p><p>Income Protection Insurance is categorized into short-term and long-term market types. Short-term income protection insurance typically covers a portion of the insured's income for a limited period, often up to two years, offering immediate financial relief in case of temporary disability or illness. In contrast, long-term income protection insurance provides benefits for an extended duration, potentially until retirement age, ensuring ongoing financial support for individuals facing chronic conditions or long-lasting disabilities, thereby safeguarding their financial stability over time.</p></p>
