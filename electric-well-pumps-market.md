<p><strong>The Electric Well Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Openwell</li><li>Borewell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric well pump market encompasses two primary types: open well pumps and borewell pumps. Open well pumps are designed for shallow water sources, typically found in large, underground reservoirs. They utilize centrifugal force to lift water to the surface. In contrast, borewell pumps are meant for deeper water sources, extracting water from narrow, drilled wells. These pumps can be submersible or vertical turbine types, efficiently handling higher depths and ensuring reliable water access for agricultural and domestic needs.</p></p>
